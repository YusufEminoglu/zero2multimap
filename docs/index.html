Advanced Multi-View Mapping

Designed for urban analysts, resilience engineers, and demographers comparing multi-dimensional spatial data.

⚏

Dynamic Layout Grids

Configure grids of 2, 3, 4, 6, or 8 panels. Supports 1x2, 2x1, 1x3, 2x2, 2x3, and 2x4 configurations instantly adjusting to fit your screen size.

⇅

Coordinated Navigation

Pan or zoom on any map panel and all other panels follow immediately. Bi-directional syncing can also be linked to your main QGIS map canvas.

⊹

Laser Crosshairs

Move your mouse in one map view and glowing neon crosshairs track your cursor coordinate across all other views and the main QGIS canvas in real time.

≣

Flexible Render Sources

Choose between syncing all active QGIS layers, locking a focused layer over a global base map, or rendering individual QGIS Map Themes.
